# Self-Assessment of AI Maturity

A short questionnaire to evaluate whether your organization has the minimum AI governance in place.

Run this assessment quarterly, or any time you significantly change your AI tool usage.

---

Eight questions. Answer Yes or No.

1. Do we have a designated AI Leader?
2. Do we maintain an AI Map?
3. Do we perform an AI Check before publishing content or making important decisions based on AI output?
4. Do we inform customers about AI use?
5. Do we have a list of data prohibited from entering AI systems?
6. Do we consult a lawyer on regulatory matters?
7. Do employees know how to report issues using AI Note?
8. Do we regularly review our AI use practices?

---

**8 / 8** — Strong foundation. Keep reviewing quarterly.

**Below 8** — Each 'No' is an action item. Start with `docs/quick-start.md`.
