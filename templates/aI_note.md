# AI Note

A lightweight internal memo for logging AI-related issues, observations, and questions.

Submit to the AI Leader. An AI Note is not a complaint — it is how the organization learns and improves.
No issue is too small to log.

---

## Fields

**Date**: [DD-MM-YYYY]

**Author**: [Name or initials]

**Priority**:
- [ ] Low — observation or suggestion, no immediate action needed
- [ ] Medium — issue affecting quality or trust, should be addressed soon
- [ ] High — security, compliance, or significant harm risk — AI Leader responds within 24 hours

**Pillar** (mark the most relevant):
- [ ] A — Accountability
- [ ] I — Inclusivity
- [ ] E — Explainability
- [ ] T — Transparency
- [ ] H — Human-Centricity
- [ ] O — Oversight
- [ ] S — Safety / Security

**Tool**: [Name of the AI tool or agent involved]

**Description**: [What happened? What did you observe? What are you concerned about? Be specific — include the prompt or output if relevant and safe to share.]

**Suggested action** (optional): [What do you think should be done?]

---

## Example

**Date**: 15-02-2026
**Author**: M.K.
**Priority**: Medium
**Tool**: ChatGPT Plus
**Description**: Generated a product description that defaulted to male pronouns when describing a target customer. The prompt did not specify gender. Output was caught before publishing.
**Suggested action**: Add a bias check reminder to the prompt template for product descriptions.

**Resolution**:
Action taken: Prompt template updated. Added to team tip sheet. Will review other product templates.
Status: Resolved

## Copy-Paste Version

Use this block to submit an AI Note in Slack, Notion, email, or any internal tool:

```
AI NOTE — [date]
Author: [name or initials]
Priority: Low / Medium / High
Pillar: [A / I / E / T / H / O / S]
Tool: [tool name]
Description: [what happened]
Suggested action: [optional]
```
